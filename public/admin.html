Approval chain

Applies to Local Conveyance, Outstation and Misc. Employee submits → HR reviews → HR routes to a final approver → on final approval the report is emailed to accounts and a WhatsApp goes to the accounts contact.

One per line: PREFIX = email. On approval, a claim's accounts email is chosen by the employee's code prefix (e.g. G2/119 → G2). Everyone else uses the default above.
Monthly CMD report

Consolidated Excel of all approved reimbursements still pending payment for a cycle (conveyance + outstation + misc). Once accounts marks a claim paid, it drops off. Email the .xlsx to the CMD with a WhatsApp download link, or download it here.

Month-end submit lock

Normally a month can only be submitted from the 1st of the next month. Turn a form's lock off to allow submitting the current month right away (useful for testing).

Employees can pick claim cycles from this month onward. Set it earlier (e.g. 2026-06) to unlock a previous cycle; set back to 2026-07 to close it. Pre-July cycles are treated as full calendar months.
